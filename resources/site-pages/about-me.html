ABOUT ME

Hi there! My name is Julius Tamale, and I'm a front-end web developer and technical writer based in Kampala, Uganda. While I have less than one year of professional experience, I have already gained strong skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, and React. Outside of work, I enjoy keeping up-to-date with the latest trends in technology, attending meetups and conferences, playing videogames, and taking a walk.
I'm eager to continue learning, growing my skills, and contributing to exciting web development projects.
